---
title: XV Transverter Series
description: Documentation for the Elecraft XV50, XV144, XV222, and XV432 external transverters

The Elecraft XV series are external transverter modules that extend K3/K3S operation to VHF and UHF bands. Available models cover 6M, 2M, 222 MHz, and 432 MHz.

The owner's manual covers operation of all XV transverter models.
